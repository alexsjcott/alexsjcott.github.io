About Me

As a First-Class Honors Computer Science MSc graduate, my journey in software development has been defined by an unwavering commitment to excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation. At Knowledge Integration, I've delved deep into the realm of cloud computing, honing my Java programming skills and embracing agile methodologies to their fullest potential. Through collaborative projects and contributions to open-source initiatives, I've not only refined my technical prowess in containerization, Git workflows, and frameworks like Grails and Micronaut but also cultivated a mindset of continuous learning and adaptation. Beyond my academic and professional achievements, my diverse background sets me apart. With over five years of experience as a Class 1 HGV Driver, complemented by roles as an Automotive Technician and a former British Army member, I bring a unique blend of communication, problem-solving, and leadership skills to the table. This fusion of experiences equips me with a holistic perspective and an innate ability to navigate complex challenges with resilience and ingenuity. Driven by a passion for making meaningful contributions, I am poised to transition into a more senior software engineering role, where I can leverage my expertise to drive impactful solutions and push the boundaries of innovation. Thank you for considering my profile—I am eager to embark on new software projects and contribute to their success.

ReShare Project

Knowledge Integration

My involvement in Project ReShare provided me with invaluable experiences in microservices architecture and containerization using Docker.
